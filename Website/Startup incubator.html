Start-up Incubator

Incubator Centre of Excellence, Govt. PG College Sector 1, Panchkula
You have a great idea but don't know where to start?
The Govt. Post Graduate College's Startup Incubator and Centre of Excellence is located in Sector 1, Panchkula.
An excellent resource for bringing your ideas to life is a business incubator.
Mentorship, shared office space, access to investors, access to their networks, and other services that a startup may require in its early stages are typical services provided by this incubator.
The Incubator established by the Department of Higher Education has provided a platform for students to enhance their entrepreneurship skills.
It plays a pivotal role in empowering rural and women entrepreneurs with its various initiatives.


It opens doors in the careers of students. It provides workplaces, mentorship, exhibits, and clientele to students aspiring to become CEOs.
Not only this but it also helps students connect with successful business owners and learn from their journeys.
It aims to create massive jobs through startups, promote innovation and attract venture capital to the nation.
Thus creating a sustainable startup ecosystem.
