Principal
Government Post Graduate College was established in 1983 with an aim to provide quality education in Humanities, Commerce and Sciences; Since then it has grown humongously in all aspects to emerge as one of the top colleges of Haryana.
It is now running 20 courses and has a well experienced faculty of 121 teachers.
The campus comprises of 8 blocks facilitated with WiFi connectivity, well equipped modern laboratories, a learning Resource Center with books and e-resources, community radio, boys hostel, well laid cinder track, gymnasium and auditorium.
An Incubator Center in the college premises helps students learn to develop their saleable talents into full fledged business.
The Placement Cell of the College in collaboration with industries, banks and academies provides training, exposure and job opportunities to the students.
The college holds pride in having produced many National and International level sports persons.
We leave no stone unturned to create every opportunity which helps impart meaningful education and extracurricular activities aiming at holistic development of our students.
